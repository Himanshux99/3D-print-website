What is a Lithophane?

A lithophane is a three-dimensional art piece that transforms ordinary photographs into stunning translucent sculptures. These unique creations are crafted with varying thickness levels that create a beautiful grayscale image when illuminated from behind, revealing depth and detail that seem almost magical.

Originally invented in Europe during the 1800s, lithophanes were painstakingly carved from porcelain by master artisans. Today, we've brought this centuries-old craft into the modern age using advanced 3D printing technology, making it possible to transform your personal photographs into affordable, personalized keepsakes.

Each lithophane is precision-printed to capture every subtle detail of your image. Whether it's a cherished family photo, a romantic moment, or a beloved pet, our lithophanes create an emotional connection that goes beyond traditional picture frames. When the light shines through, your memories literally come to life in a way that never fails to amaze.
